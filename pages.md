# List of Pages 

## Authentication & User 
*These pages are all related to authentication or the user in some form or another.*

- Registration 
- Log in 
- Profile; ability to view yours/others profiles 
- Settings; ability to change your own settings 
- Notifications; ability to view your unread notifications 

## Committees 
*These pages are all related to managing, creating, and joining specific committees.* 

- Creating a committee 
- Joining a committee 
- Accepting committee invite 
- Viewing committee information 
  - General committee information; name, description, member count 
  - Users in the committee, with their roles
  - Committee-specific motions 
- Managing a committee 


## Motions
*These pages are all related to creating, viewing, and voting for motions.* 

- Motions homepage; with all of the (active) motions listed 
- Create a motion
- Motion detail (modal dialog)
  - Description
  - Comments
  - Voting
- Motions history, ability to sort by status and search 

## Administration 
*These pages are all related to administration of the platform itself.**

- Main admin panel
  - Approve committees/manually create them 
  - Approve user registration 
  - Managing community info, and specific details such as how long it takes for a motion to end 
  - Managing users, manually removing them or granting them elevated permissions with roles, i.e. have to be a chair to create a committee
